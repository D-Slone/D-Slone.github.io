The Mission

Our mission was simple, return to Earth...

On our way we ran into an unexpected asteroid belt that severely damaged the ship. The main engine and top thruster are causing the ship to gain speed uncontrollably and at a downward angle. Our only way to survive is to engage the bottom thruster at max capacity at the right time to avoid the asteroids. Don't get too close or you risk crashing the ship, we can't take anymore damage! Our lives rest with you Captain, get us home safe!


Instructions

  • Click the start button to begin the game.
  • Use the Right Arrow key (->) to engage the bottom thruster forcing the ship upward.
  • Click the restart button to play again.
  • Dodge the oncoming asteroids and get your crew home safely!
